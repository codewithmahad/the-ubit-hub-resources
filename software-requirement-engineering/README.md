> **Software Requirement Engineering (SE453)** focuses on the principles, methods, and tools used to elicit, analyze, specify, validate, and manage software requirements. This repository centralizes all required reading materials, structured notes, and official documentation to streamline exam preparation and coursework.

## Recommended Study Flow

1. **Understand Scope:** Review the `Course Outline` to map out the topics required for your specific exam or assignment.
2. **Primary Learning:** Use the `Comprehensive Notes` as your baseline for studying. 
3. **Cross-Reference:** When a concept in the notes feels unclear, consult the specific `Extracted Chapter` for the foundational theory.
4. **Deep Dive:** Only open the full `Reference Books` when you need further context outside of the extracted chapters.
